Crockpot Garlic Parm Chicken and Potatoes

It's crockpot garlic parmesan chicken! I can't imagine a more beautiful thing! When I had it with potatoes, everything changed!

Back to homepage
Ingredients
  • 3 (separate)tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 lbs bone-in,skin-on chicken thighs
  • Kosher salt
  • Fresh ground black pepper
  • Fresh chopped parsley
  • 1 lb baby red potatoes, quartered
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softed
  • 5 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 tbsp. fresh thyme
  • 2 tbsp. grated parm(but really as much as you want because it's grated parm so just measure with your heart
Preparation
  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Add chicken, season with salt and pepper, and sear until golden(around 3 minutes per side)
  3. In a crockpot, toss the potatoes with 2 tbsp oil, and the rest of the ingredients. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  4. Add chicken and cook on high for 4 hours or low for 8 hours,until potatoes are tender and chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Garnish with as much parmesan as you want, and serve!
